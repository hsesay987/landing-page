Home care Agency Management Platform with EVV

***CONFIDENTIAL: This is not Open Source***

A comprehensive home care agency management platform designed to streamline scheduling, client data, billing, and Electronic Visit Verification (EVV) compliance. It includes role-based portals for system administrators and agency users, with features for managing clients, employees, visits, and invoices. The frontend is built with modern React (Next.js) architecture and integrated with backend services (Node.js, Express, PostgreSQL with Prisma). A key goal is future integration with platforms like Sandata to sync visit data for state reporting and continuity if agencies migrate away. The system is being developed with scalability, compliance, and ease of use in mind.
